A paragraph is a distinct unit of writing, typically composed of multiple sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It serves to organize and structure written work, making it easier for readers to follow the author's train of thought. Paragraphs are fundamental to longer pieces of writing, such as essays, articles, and even emails, providing clarity and flow. Here's a more detailed look at paragraphs: Structure: A basic paragraph usually includes a topic sentence (introducing the main idea), supporting sentences (providing details and evidence), and sometimes a concluding sentence (summarizing the main point or transitioning to the next paragraph)
